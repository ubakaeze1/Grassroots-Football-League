About Us

The Nigeria Grassroots Football Competitions body is dedicated to discovering and promoting football talents across local communities in Idemili North and South. We create competitive platforms that empower young players, clubs, and academies to thrive and gain visibility both locally and nationally.

Our mission is to develop football from the grassroots level, providing opportunities for young talents to showcase their skills, and creating pathways to professional football careers.

Competition Categories

  • 5-A-Side Football Competition
  • 7-A-Side Football Competition
  • U-13 Boys Championship
  • U-15 Boys Championship
  • U-17 Boys Championship
  • U-15 & U-17 Girls League
  • Open-Age Community Clubs League
  • Academy Challenge Cup
  • Inter-LGA Youth Tournament
  • Annual Grassroots Football Festival
